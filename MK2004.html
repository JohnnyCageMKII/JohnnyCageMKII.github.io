Mortal Kombat: Deception (2004)

Overview
Mortal Kombat: Deception is the follow up from it's predicesor Tonament Edition. Its story centers on the revival of the Evil Dragon King Onaga, who attempts to conquer the realms featured in the series after defeating the sorcerers Quan Chi and Shang Tsung, the main antagonists in the previous game, and the Thunder God Raiden, defender of Earthrealm.
