Mortal Kombat 11 (2019)

Overview
Mortal Kombat 11 made in 2019 is a new edition to the Mortal Kombat series given us the viewers and gamers a great story to work with. The plot follows after Shinnok's defeat, Dark Raiden plans to protect Earthrealm by destroying all of its enemies and making an example of Shinnok, decapitating him. This latter act inspires Kronika, the keeper of time, to rewrite history to undo Raiden's interference and erase him from existence.
