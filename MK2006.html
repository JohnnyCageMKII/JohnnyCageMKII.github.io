Mortal Kombat: Armageddon (2006)

Overview
Mortal Kombat: Armageddon is the seventh installment in the Mortal Kombat series. The story follows a warrior named Taven after being awakened who goes on a journey to defeat his evil brother leading up to the Armageddon war to determine the fate of the Mortal Kombat universe.
