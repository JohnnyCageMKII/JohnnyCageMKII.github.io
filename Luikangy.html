Lui Kang

Overview
Lui Kang is a former Shaolin Monk and undisputed champion of the Mortal Kombat Tournament! He is the eternal "God of Fire", protector of Earthrealm, and the former "Keeper of Time". Liu Kang has remained one of the most recognizable and memorable characters in the Mortal Kombat series and even today will be recognized for his slkill and strength.
